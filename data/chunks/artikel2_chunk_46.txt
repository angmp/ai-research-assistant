In summary, by dividing the flight mission into stages and calculating the power
requirements for each stage, the real-time discharge parameters of the battery at different
flight stages can be determined, which in turn allows for the construction of a time-varying
discharge curve. These results provide key input data for the subsequent development of
the Bernardi thermal model for the battery.
